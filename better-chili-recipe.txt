Ingredients
- 2 lbs lean ground beef
- 1 can (28 oz) salt-free crushed tomatoes
- 1/3 cup chili powder
- 2 cans (16 oz each) red kidney beans, drained
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 large chopped onion (about 1 1/4 cups)
- 3 minced garlic cloves
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp black pepper

Directions
1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium-high heat and saute the onion and garlic for 5 minutes, or until tender.
2. Add the ground beef and brown for 8 to 10 minuts, or until no pink remains; drain off excess liquid.
3. Dump in the remaining ingredients; mix well.
4. Reduce the heat to low; cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
